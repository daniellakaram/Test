Leading authentically in one-on-one relationships


  • Embodies and champions organizational values in daily interactions and leads with same behaviors as expected by the team.
  • Maintains composure under pressure or during times of uncertainty helping teams stay focused, productive, and engaged.

  • Demonstrates integrity and accountability by acknowledging mistakes, seeking input, and following through on commitments.
  • Interacts with others honestly, respectfully, and with emotional intelligence, especially during challenges and times of change. Intentionally works to build psychological safety.

Experiential Learning


Lead With Your Values Daily
  • Identify ways to live the values through your work and intentionally make and execute plans on living those values.
Make Space for Real Conversations
  • During one-on-one or team meetings, ensure to allow time to listen to and respectfully address questions and concerns.
Show Your Team Your Human Side
  • Identify ways to show vulnerability to your team – share challenges, own up to mistakes, seek feedback, etc.

Reflection


Model What You Expect From Others
  • Am I modeling the behaviors and standards that I expect from my team?
Practice Radical Transparency
  • Am I transparent with my team about goals, challenges, and expectations?
Build a Culture of Candor
  • How do I ensure I create an environment where others are comfortable sharing ideas or concerns?

Feedback


Close the Gap on Living Values
  • How can I better model organizational values?
Lead With Integrity, Not Just Intent
  • How do I demonstrate honest, integrity, and transparency through my interactions with my team?
Be the Leader Others Trust First
  • How approachable and trustworthy do I appear when discussing challenges, giving feedback, or during uncertainty?
